Lostify is a unique platform designed specifically for students at KIET Group of Institutions to facilitate hitchhiking and ride-sharing within the campus community. The idea behind Lostify is to make travel more convenient and cost-effective for students, whether they are commuting to and from college, going on weekend trips, or even running small errands. By connecting students traveling in the same direction, Lostify not only helps reduce travel costs but also promotes a more sustainable, eco-friendly way to commute.
